Multiplicity

I co-founded Multiplicity, a digital curation of articles on the internet's place in our lives on both a personal and socio-political level as young people in different parts of the world. We critically examine the internet's influence and explore ways to build a healthier and more equitable internet. Our team came from 16 countries: Palestine, Jordan, Egypt, Lebanon, Nepal, Bosnia and Herzegovina, New Zealand, South Korea, the US, Hungary, Northern Ireland, India, Colombia, Sudan, Iraq, and China.

In addition to managing the project, providing editorial support, and co-developing the website as a whole, I lead the development and design of the article web pages. Here are my favorite 3 articles that I designed and built to be interactive, informative, and fun:

1. How to Mute a Country (words by Dervla O'Brien)

2. Delivery Nation: Challenging the Food Delivery Monopoly in South Korea (words by Jihyun Kim)

3. Reflections on Exile in the Digital Age (words by Lujain Ibrahim)

Languages & libraries: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (JSON, Leaflet, Tabulator, ApexCharts, & more...)

2020-2021

Coptico

Coptico Technologies was a fake company selling innovative products meant to solve the Arab world's peace, coexistence, gender inequality, and social solidarity issues. It was the center of an art exhbit critiquing the productization and commercialization of social issues. The exhbit consisted of the products in their physical form, short mockumentaries to advertize each product, and animated chatbots of all the characters in the mockumentaries. Using Sitepal to create avatars of the actors and using the Pandorabots API, I adapted 4 film scripts into 300+ questions and answers in Artificial Intelligence Markup Language (AIML) and embedded the bots onto the exhibit's website. Each of the 6 animated bots were placed on an tablet in a designated area for vistors to talk to them and ask questions about the products as well as the state of the Arab world today. Unfortunately, the bots are no longer live but here are a few pictures/videos and the Github repository.

Languages & libraries: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (using APIs), AIML

I completed my group undergraduate senior thesis on deep learning applications for health data. We co-authored and published a paper with our results in IEEE Access.
