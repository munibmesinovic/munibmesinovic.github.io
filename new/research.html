research

My main research interests lie at the intersection of human-algorithm interactions and AI fairness, accountability, and transparency. My doctoral research looks at how end-user agency exercised through algorithmic governance approaches that increase {design-based, information-based, and market-based} control influences the outcomes of human-AI interactions.

I'd love to speak to and work with more people interested in or working on similar things. Reach out if that's you :)
